Blockchain based service engine for Tangible Asset Management
• Goal: to design, develop and demonstrate a service-engine using blockchain technology for Land Property Management. This was final year project sponsored by Persistent Systems Ltd.

• Salient features: (a) registering, selling, buying, mortgaging a property, (b) maintaining a chain of custody, (c) property auctions, all implemented through Smart Contracts

• Technology: Ethereum, Solidity, HTML, CSS, JavaScript, Node.js and Web3 API

• Publication: Blockchain based Service Engine for Tangible Asset Management. In the Proceedings of IEEE International Conference on Electrical, Electronics, Computers, Communication, Mechanical and Computing, India, 28-29 January 2018, pp. 232-237.

• Accolades: The Best Project Award in two inter-collegiate National level symposia
